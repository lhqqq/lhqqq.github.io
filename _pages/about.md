My research focuses on **medical AI**, **biometrics**, and **large language models**. I received the IJCB 2023 [Best Student Paper Award](https://ijcb2023.ieee-biometrics.org/award-winners/) and won the [8th Sclera Segmentation and Recognition Benchmarking Competition (SSRBC 2023)](https://sites.google.com/hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in/ssrbc2023/home?pli=1).
